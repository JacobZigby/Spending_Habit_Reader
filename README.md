# Spending_Habit_Reader

<b>Objective</b>: to build a time series module to properly predict someone's monthly spending habits, (example the percentage of money spent on essentials, bills, and non essentials), while also keeping note of regular outliers such as birthdays or yearly renewals, which will be based on credit/debit card estatments of the past months
<br>If time permits it, add more members to be analyzed, and create a group objective model to see what a group mindset could look like, and finally build comparison models that would meet ideal habits one could wish to achieve, wether from other peoples spending habits or one bulit soley on personaslised numbers
